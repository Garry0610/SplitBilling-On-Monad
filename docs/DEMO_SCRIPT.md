# Demo Video Script (≤ 3 minutes)

Keep it tight — judges click "submit" twice to check it's real, so show real
transactions, not a script or hardcoded values.

**0:00–0:20 — The problem**
"Every time I split a bill with friends, someone forgets to pay, or we argue
about who already sent money back. There's no shared record."

**0:20–0:40 — What SplitBill does**
One-sentence pitch + show the landing page / connect wallet.

**0:40–1:30 — Live demo, real transaction**
1. Create a bill with 2–3 participant addresses and shares.
2. Switch wallet / show a second participant paying their share — a real
   signed transaction, show the wallet popup and the tx confirming.
3. Refresh the page — show the paid status updating from on-chain state
   (not a local variable).

**1:30–2:00 — Settlement**
Show the bill flipping to "Settled" once everyone has paid, and the
organizer's balance reflecting the transfer.

**2:00–2:30 — Contract on explorer**
Pull up the Monad testnet explorer, show the deployed contract address and
the transaction history — proof it's really on-chain.

**2:30–3:00 — Close**
Recap the problem/solution in one sentence, mention team name.

## Checklist before recording
- [ ] Contract deployed and verified on Monad Testnet (or Mainnet)
- [ ] No mock/hardcoded data anywhere in the flow
- [ ] At least 2 real wallets used to show multi-participant flow
- [ ] Explorer link ready to show contract + tx history
